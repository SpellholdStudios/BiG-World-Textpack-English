I - Mod Introduction :

The exiles of Silverymoon is a quest mod that adds a contact with a mysterious new group. The chance to get a lot of new encounters throughout the game in well-known places but also in new places since the mod offers a new fully customized area. The quest will start automatically when you start a new game as long as you have a neutral or evil alignment (for consistency reasons I was forced to do so). The mod takes 1140 lines, what fun you could experience for a while and as you might notice a number of characters were doubled (129 replies in total). So much for the small description, I wish you a pleasant playing and I hope that this first version will make you want to play with the new that will see the day...



II - Install Instructions :

** It is VERY highly recommended that you install the latest available patch for Baldur's Gate II - Throne of Bhaal. If you own Throne of Bhaal, please note that you do not need to patch Shadows of Amn. **
** The installation of BG2 Fixpack of Gibberlings Three is, as well, strongly recommended. **
** I remind you that the expansion Throne of Bhaal is needed to take advantage of the Exiles of Silverymoon. **

Detailed Instructions :
1. Ensure that no software using the resources of Baldur's Gate II is running. This includes the game itself, utilities as NearInfinity or ShadowKeeper and the installation of another mod.
2. Extract the archive.
3. Click Setup - Devin.exe and let Weidu do its job!
4. Now, pleasant playing!

VI - FAQ :



I do not have access to the new city, is this normal ?

It depends. If you refused to follow Jackomo or Devin at one point or another you do not actually get (or no more) access to the new city. An alternative component is planned, it will be released in a few months and let you explore a new part of the history.

I want to recruit Bardick but I can not, is this normal ?

Yes, this is normal. It was indeed a question in my early to recruit Bardick but as the contruction I saw things differently and now I think it's slightly more interesting to develop him as a non recuitable character. As you will see in the next versions, I hope...

Where is Sidge ?

Sidge is by Renal Bloodscalp in the basement. And he's a.... THIEF ! It seems logical, right ?
