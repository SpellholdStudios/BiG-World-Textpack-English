I - Introduction of Mod :

Pirate Kit is a Weidu mod for Baldur's Gate II Shadows of Amn or/and its add-on Throne of Bhaal. As its name suggests, it offers you to make a real pirate of the Sword Coast out of your main character. Like any respectable pirate, he carries a greedy and talkative parrot ; of a personal treasure chest and a lot of other surprises.

 

II - Installation Instructions :

** It is HIGHLY recommended to install the latest available patch for Baldur's Gate II - Throne of Bhaal. If you have installed Throne of Bhaal, please note that you do not need to patch Shadows of Amn. **
** The installation of BG2 Fixpack on Gibberlings Three is also strongly recommended. **
** I remind you that the expansion Throne of Bhaal is required to benefit from Pirate Kit Mod. **

Detailed instructions :
1. Ensure that no running program uses the resources of Baldur's Gate II. This includes the game itself, utilities such as NearInfinity or ShadowKeeper and the installation of another mod.
2. Extract the archive.
3. Click Setup-Pirate.exe and let yourself be guided by Weidu instructions. (You only have to click again Setup-Pirate.exe to uninstall the mod).
4. And now, play well!

 

III - Compatibility and Known Bugs :

Pirate Kit Mod is compatibile with BGT as well as with all large mods like Check The bodies, The Darkest Day, Shadows Over Soubar or Tortured Souls insofar they are installed before the kit. Pirate Kit Mod is also compatible with most minor mods. However, it is advisable to install Pirate Kit Mod after other eventual kit or quest mods.

Warning : Pirate Kit Mod is exclusively reserved for the main character. It is not compatible in multiplayer.

VII - Version History :

Version 1.1 – October, 28 2009 :

- Fixed bugs and inconsistencies.

- Option to choose an innate regional gift during installation.

- Option to select  the race of the parrot (Scarlet macaw or Blue-and-yellow macaw) during installation.

- Addition of items (some magical) retrieved by the parrot.

- Added a disadvantage to counterbalance the regional gifts : -1 to reputation per level between level 8 and 22.

- Improvement of the option to call a pirate crew ; using a call horn rather that a spell and improvement of visual effects.

- New custom portraits available during the creation of the pirate character.

- Visual improvement of some bams (graphics of items).

- Penalty to charisma at -4 instead of -5.

- Addition of some interjections when casting certain spells.

 

Version 1.0 – May, 21 2009 :

- First version of Pirate Kit Mod.
